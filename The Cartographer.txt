mob 600

names cartographer old-cartographer man
short desc - the old cartographer
long desc - A cartographer is busy mapping at his desk.
Description -
The old cartographer is frantically transposing his thoughts from mind to
material.  He cannot risk forgetting his paths from past adventures and must cartograph
before his mind lets them loose and they are lost forever.

/f

Room 3155
====
The Cartographer's Room
   This cozy little room on the main floor of the inn belongs to a skilled
cartographer.  It has been meticulously filled from top to bottom, side to side,
on every wall with books and scrolls.  The room is small and full but organized.

/fi

Location
=========
exit s -> 3052
North of 3052. I was thinking this cartographer could be staying at the in during his travels
and not have a "permanent" residence in Mielikki

redit 3052, exit n -> 3155

Features
========
Randomly emotes (or on enter)
trig 600, random 5

mecho The Cartographer mutters something about his memory fading.
